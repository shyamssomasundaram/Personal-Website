Bioinformatics Scientist - Data Modeler Intern at The National Marrow Donor Program (May 2014 ‐ Present)

The National Marrow Donor Program is a non-profit organization which manages and maintains the Be The Match registry, the largest and most diverse registry for bone marrow transplantation in the world. It has been accredited for performing transplantations to cure blood related cancers like Leukemia and Lymphoma for the past 25 years.

As a Data Modeler Intern, I am responsible for assisting in developing successful data modeling technical solutions for the Bioinformatics Research Department of the NMDP. This includes responsibilities (with supervision) of collecting, analyzing, reviewing, documenting, and communicating business needs between NMDP Bioinformatics Research staff and Center for International Blood and Marrow Transplant Research (CIBMTR) IT. This also includes responsibility of providing enterprise applications development and database modeling changes to existing applications as specified.
